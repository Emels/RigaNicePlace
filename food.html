Best Places to Eat in Riga

If you go to Riga, here’s what you cannot miss!

1. LIDO VĒRMANĪTIS

A great place to eat a huge meal for cheap, this restaurant looks like medieval Europe and interwar Latvia collided and produced a unique space decorated with ethnic symbols, stone and stained glass. Bop about to local 'umm-paa-paa' music and help yourself to Baltic cuisine as well as salad and fruit bars. When it gets crowded at lunch time, it also opens the cellar to provide more space. If you're lucky enough to get a seat near a window on the ground floor you'll be rewarded with a great view of the park.

2. ROZENGRĀLS

The restaurant ROZENGRĀLS is located in the centre of Old Riga on the Rozena Street. These premises were mentioned in the scripts in 1293 as the most ancient wine-vault and festival place of the Riga City Council! In the largest room of the restaurant with a 4-meter high arches is still the only well in Riga and medieval fragment of the first defensive wall, built in 1201! In 5 halls, in a medieval atmosphere at the same time we will be able to regale 150 guests with exquisite dishes prepared according to old recipes!

3. LIDOJOŠĀ VARDE

Lidojošā varde is one of the oldest restaurants in the quiet centre of Riga. The menu of this establishment will disappoint neither adults nor children.Various salads, sandwiches, soups and desserts will be offered for a light meal. But if you want a more substantial meal, you will be able to choose pasta, or a meal prepared with meat or fish. While the little visitors of the restaurant await their food, they will be able to enjoy drawing or colouring. Presentation of food for the children has especially been thought of; it will be decorated in a playful and colourful manner.
